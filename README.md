# 🎬 Smart Movie Finder

**Smart Movie Finder** is a **fun and interactive Streamlit web app** that helps you discover movies based on your **mood**, not just the genre. Whether you feel happy, thoughtful, or intense, this app recommends the perfect movie for your vibe!  

---

## 🌟 Features

- Browse movies by **Genre** and **Mood**.  
- See **movie posters** with titles, genre, and mood.  
- **Dark modern theme** for a sleek look.  
- **Hover effect** on cards for interactive browsing.  
- **Quick and easy** recommendations at your fingertips.
